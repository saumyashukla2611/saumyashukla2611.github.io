About

The Short Version

I thrive on solving hard problems. My career has been built on understanding how organizations work — and making them work better. From strategic advisory to hands-on execution, I bring a bias for clarity, rigor, and impact.

I've always been drawn to the space where technology meets business transformation. Whether it's building data-driven strategies, leading cross-functional teams, or designing systems that scale, I believe the best work happens when analytical thinking meets creative problem-solving.

Beyond the professional, I'm a lifelong learner. I write about technology, strategy, and the ideas that shape how we work. This site is my space for thinking out loud.
