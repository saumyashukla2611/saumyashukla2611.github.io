Chickpea Salad

Crispy baked chickpeas tossed with fresh greens and tangy chutneys — a vibrant, flavor-packed salad that comes together in minutes.

chickpeas lettuce green pani puri chutney tamarind chutney green onions lemon

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (canned or dried, soaked overnight)
  • Lettuce leaves, washed and torn
  • Green pani puri chutney (mint-coriander chutney)
  • Tamarind chutney (sweet & tangy)
  • Green onions, finely sliced
  • Lemon, freshly squeezed
  • Salt, pepper, and chaat masala to taste
  • Olive oil or cooking spray

Method

1. Prepare the Chickpeas

If using dried chickpeas, soak them overnight and boil until tender but still firm. If using canned, drain and rinse thoroughly. Pat the chickpeas completely dry with a kitchen towel — this is the key to getting them crispy.

2. Season & Bake

Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F). Toss the dried chickpeas with a drizzle of olive oil, salt, pepper, and a generous pinch of chaat masala. Spread them in a single layer on a baking sheet. Bake for 25–30 minutes, shaking the tray halfway through, until golden and crispy.

3. Prep the Greens

While the chickpeas are baking, wash and tear the lettuce into bite-sized pieces. Finely slice the green onions — both the white and green parts.

4. Assemble

Arrange the lettuce on a plate or in a wide bowl. Add the warm, crispy chickpeas on top. Scatter the sliced green onions generously.

5. Dress It Up

Drizzle the green pani puri chutney and tamarind chutney over the salad. Squeeze fresh lemon juice on top. Toss gently or leave it layered — both work beautifully.

Notes

The magic here is the contrast: crispy warm chickpeas against cool fresh lettuce, the tangy-sweet tamarind against the punchy green chutney, and a bright hit of lemon to tie it all together. Best served immediately while the chickpeas are still crunchy.
